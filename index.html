MARKET PERFORMANCE

As of the end of April 2025, BYD's cumulative new energy vehicle (NEV) sales exceeded 11.9 million units. In the first half of 2023, BYD
ranked among the global top 10 in new car sales. On November 18, 2024, BYD became the world's first automaker to produce 10 million
NEVs.
